Welcome. This is my blog.

They call it the curse of knowledge: when you've known something for so long that you can't remember ever not knowing it. You suddenly find you've become a bad teacher, impatient because you can't understand how someone else could have so much trouble with something you've seemingly always known.

I got started in software later than many of my friends, so I can appreciate just how difficult the learning curve can be. I hope to use this blog to ease some of the hardest parts in the hopes of helping someone else making the same journey. And to teach myself just how much more I have to learn.
