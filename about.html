Hi. My name is Prateek Sahay.

I'm currently earning my MBA/MEng at UC Berkeley, and I've always been passionate about robotics engineering. Before Berkeley, I was at Uber for a year working on self-driving cars. Before that, I was at Sikorsky Aircraft for 4 years working on autonomous aircraft for the DARPA ALIAS program. Prior to that, I graduated from Worcester Polytechnic Institute in 2015 with two Bachelor's degrees in Robotics and Mechanical Engineering.

In my free time, I practice web design, electronics, programming, typography, and woodworking. I've always held a strong belief in the importance of balancing engineering sciences with vibrant arts, so I've constantly drawn to both. I'm especially attracted to environments where I am continually learning.

I also enjoy giving back to the community through volunteer work; I've volunteered with FIRST Robotics, performed magic shows, worked exhibits at the Museum of Science in Boston, and done graphic design work for Essence of India, an annual celebration of classical Indian culture and heritage in my hometown.

My interests are ever-expanding—I enjoy reading, traveling, building, photographing, driving, building sound systems, playing table tennis, and having chill sessions with friends. I'm also a fan of usability and front-end design.

About the Website

I designed this website on my own and coded it on top of Twitter's Bootstrap templates and the Jekyll framework. It was my first foray into HTML and CSS, and it's been a lot of fun. Thanks to the powers of Bootstrap, this website is fully optimized for your mobile display, as well. Enjoy!
